# Business Questions

## Project Context

Olist operates as a Brazilian e-commerce marketplace connecting customers, sellers, products, payments, reviews, and delivery operations. This project uses SQL and Power BI to analyse customer behaviour, order journeys, delivery performance, satisfaction, revenue, and retention.

The goal is to identify product and operational opportunities that could improve customer experience, repeat purchase behaviour, and marketplace performance.

---

## Core Business Questions

### 1. Customer Journey and Order Funnel

- How many orders are created, approved, shipped, delivered, cancelled, or unavailable?
- Where does the order journey lose the most volume?
- What percentage of orders successfully reach delivery?
- How long does it take from purchase to approval, carrier handoff, and customer delivery?

### 2. Revenue and Commercial Performance

- What is total revenue based on item price and payment value?
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which states or cities contribute most to revenue?
- Which sellers generate the most revenue?
- Which payment methods are most commonly used?

### 3. Customer Retention and Repeat Purchase

- What percentage of customers place more than one order?
- How does repeat purchase behaviour vary by state, product category, or payment method?
- Which customer cohorts show stronger or weaker repeat behaviour?
- Are there signs of retention decay over time?

### 4. Delivery and Operations Performance

- What percentage of delivered orders arrive late compared with estimated delivery date?
- Which states, sellers, or categories have the highest late delivery rate?
- What is the average delivery time?
- Are some product categories operationally harder to fulfil?

### 5. Customer Satisfaction and Reviews

- What is the average review score?
- How do review scores vary by delivery status, delay, product category, and seller?
- Do late deliveries correlate with lower customer review scores?
- Which categories or sellers create the most dissatisfaction risk?

### 6. Product and Marketplace Insights

- Which product categories combine high revenue with poor satisfaction?
- Which sellers create strong revenue but weak delivery/review performance?
- Which regions show strong demand but operational weaknesses?
- What actions should Olist prioritise to improve customer experience and marketplace health?
